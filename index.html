FAQ

Questions we get
all the time.

What industries do you work with? +

We work with ambitious SMEs across multiple sectors: construction and home improvement, IT and software, healthcare and clinics, fitness and pilates, corporate services and accounting, landscaping, permanent makeup and aesthetics, and professional B2B services. If you are a growing business in the UAE that needs more qualified leads, we can likely help.

How are you different from a typical agency? +

Most agencies send you leads and consider their job done. We care about what happens after the lead arrives. Are they the right person? Do they have the budget? Have they already seen your work and trust you? We build the whole journey, not just the top of it. So by the time someone contacts you, the hard work is already done.

When can I expect to see my first results? +

Most of our clients start receiving qualified enquiries within 2 to 4 weeks of their campaign going live. It depends on your location, offer, and budget. We set realistic expectations from day one and we do not launch until we are confident the setup is solid.

What if I already have marketing running? +

Good. That gives us something to work with. We will take a look at what you are running, what is performing and what is not, and figure out the fastest path to better results. Sometimes it is a small fix. Sometimes it is a full rebuild. We will be honest about what needs to change.

Do I need a big advertising budget to get started? +

We work with businesses at different spend levels. On our first call, we will tell you straight what ad budget we would recommend based on your goals and market. If the numbers do not make sense for you, we will say so. We would rather lose the client than overpromise and underdeliver.

What does the onboarding process actually look like? +

After our strategy call we collect your brand assets, build the creatives and copy, set up your ads manager and tracking, and get everything ready to launch. You are updated at each stage. Most campaigns are live within 10 to 14 days of us starting work together.
